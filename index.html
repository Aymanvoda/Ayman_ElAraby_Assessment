⚠ ProblemCS3 — Trainer Reporting: Root Causes
No Deadline Ownership
Why Reports Are LATE — Cause 1
Deadlines exist but are not enforced — treated as optional admin, not a performance KPI
When reporting is optional, it is the first thing dropped under pressure. It must be a non-negotiable deliverable.
No Template
Why Reports Are INACCURATE — Cause 1
Every trainer uses a different format — no standardised locked template with mandatory fields
Without a template, accuracy depends on each trainer's interpretation. Different formats = incomparable, unreliable data.
No Training
Why Reports Are INACCURATE — Cause 2
Trainers were never formally taught how to fill the reports correctly
Expecting accuracy without training is unrealistic. Accuracy issues are, in part, a training problem.
No Consequence
Why It Continues
Missing a deadline has no visible consequence for any of the 4 non-compliant trainers
The behaviour repeats because nothing changes. Until there is a defined, enforced consequence, the pattern will continue.
